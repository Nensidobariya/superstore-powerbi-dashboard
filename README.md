## Key Business Insights Discovered

| Insight | Finding | Business Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Discount Impact | 40%+ discount = negative profit | Revise discount policy |
| Top Region | West region leads with $0.73M sales | Focus expansion here |
| Best Category | Copiers = highest profit margin | Increase inventory |
| Risk Area | Machines sub-category losing money | Review pricing |
